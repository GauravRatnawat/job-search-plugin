03 — Scoring

Honest fit,
five dimensions

Every job is scored against your resume across five weighted dimensions. Scores are honest — a 45/100 stretch role is useful information. Synonyms are resolved. Fresher projects count as experience.

Final grade maps to a probability category: A/B ship immediately, C needs a tailored resume, D is aspirational, F is skipped.
